WHO - Cough Management in Adults
Cough is a common reflex action that clears the throat of mucus or foreign irritants.
most coughs are caused by a viral infection (like a cold or flu) and clear up within 3 weeks.
Self-care for cough:
- Honey and lemon can help soothe a cough.
- Stay hydrated.
- Rest.
Antibiotics are rarely needed for coughs because they do not kill viruses.
Warning signs (Red Flags) for Cough:
- Coughing up blood.
- Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing.
- Chest pain.
- Unexplained weight loss.
- Cough lasting more than 3 weeks.
If you experience these warning signs, seek medical attention immediately.
High Risk Groups:
- Smokers.
- People with chronic lung conditions (COPD, Asthma).
- Immunocompromised individuals.
